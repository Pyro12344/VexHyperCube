Design & Performance

Our robot was designed and built entirely by our team in the school's Makerspace using VEX V5 components.

It features a four-motor drive system optimized for acceleration and precise maneuvering across the 12√ó12 ft field, enabling rapid transitions between Loaders, Goals, and Park Zones. At the front, a dual-intake mechanism allows for quick and reliable collection of blocks from the field or directly from the Loaders.

The current version achieves a consistent balance between power, accuracy, and durability, designed to adapt to both offensive strategies (rapid block scoring) and defensive situations (zone control).
